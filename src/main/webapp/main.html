Tracking Golf Statistics for You

So this Application can track your Golf Stats?

The golf stat recorder is designed to track any casual player stats when that player comes to a course we have in our database. We are looking to grow our database with many courses so each player that visit a course in our database will have a memory for playing at that course. With the golf stat recorder, you can create a player account, upload a Scorecard, view the courses available in our database, view players who have created accounts, and view Scorecards in our system for each player who have played at a course we support.
